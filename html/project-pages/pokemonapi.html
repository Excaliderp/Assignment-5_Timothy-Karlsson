Being the nerd that I am, i chose Pokémon API. First i did not want to, since out lector used it to teach us API, but it was so ridiculously well developed, I just had to play around with it. I made it both as a randomizer and also asking for user input to find a Pkémon based on their Pokédex enrty number.
